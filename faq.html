Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can your product be used on relaxed hair?

    Yes, all our products can be used for any hair type, including relaxed(permed) hair.

  • How do I use your products?

    All products come with instructions on how to use them. You can find instructions on all product pages on our website as well.

  • How do I know I am allergic to your products?

    Our products are all naturally sourced but we cannot guarantee that people would not be allergic to them. We advise that you check from our ingridients list, do your own research and consult your doctor.

  • What payment options are available?

    You can make payments via card(Visa/Mastercard) or Mobile Money.

  • Do you ship internationally?

    Yes, we do ship internationally via DHL. Please check for DHL's availability in your country before placing an order.

  • How long does it take to receive an order?

    Shipping within Ghana takes 2-3 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on your location.

  • How do I track my order?

    After placing your order, you will receive an email notification with information on your order which includes a unique order and tracking number. A link to the tracking website would be included in the email for your convienience
